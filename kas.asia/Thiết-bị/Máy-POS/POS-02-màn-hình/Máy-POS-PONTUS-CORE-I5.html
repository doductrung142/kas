CPU: Intel® Core i5-4250U Dual-Core, 3M

RAM: 4GB Standard, DDR3L 1333 MT/s, Socket 1 x SODIMM (Max. 16GB)

Storage: mSATA 128GB

OS: Option (Windows 7, Windows 8.1, Windows 10 IoT Enterprise, POS Ready 7)

Màn hình: 15 inch TFT LCD, LED Backlight

Màu sắc: Đen/ Xám đen (Black/ Matt Dark Grey)
