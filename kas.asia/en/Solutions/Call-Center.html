Call Center - Multi-channel communication management for Business

Professional call center solution, help to optimize operating costs, improve customer care efficiency, increase competitiveness for businesses in the 4.0 era


Reduce costs
Save 50% on calling charges, free internal calls, and break-through sales volume by increasing the quality of customer care service
Convenient call management
Optimal call separation: When the call center receives a call from a new customer, the call center will store the customer's phone number, when the old customer calls back, the call center will query the data, then transfer the call to staff in chargeâ¦
The system analyzes and evaluates the call quality of employees, automatically reports in real time. Attach a ticket after every call
Connect with CRM system
Connect to the Enterprise's CRM system, allow access to information on incoming customers on the staff's working interface
With multi-channel care support: Facebook, call center, Website, Zalo ... this brings a limitless experience for users upon their interaction with businesses. Thus, customers are willing to come back for additional purchase
Cross-platform support
Employees can use it on the web platform, in addition, they can use the Mobile App to perform their work, it is supported on Android and iOS platforms.
Flexibly change the call receiving from customers on the Web or mobile app, optimize working tools for employees
Management Dashboard
Monitor all incoming / outgoing calls status in real time, manage detailed call history, outgoing calls and missed calls on an overall Dashboard.
Record all support calls. Enterprises will be supported to assess the personnel quality and calls with customers
