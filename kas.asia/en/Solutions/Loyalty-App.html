Customer data management

Customer data is a business asset, especially in hospitality industry and other types of business models in 4.0 periods.

Stored and Specified by business scale in the system with equivalent policies and deals.



Register now



Marketing with zero-cost

Mobile app creates a powerful communicate channel between business and users. All customer's data is always in the system to optimize customer care actions

With advertising new products and promotions via app and push notifications, business can save lots of ads-work fee as well as finding a new customer fee. All ads-work will be run automatically and effectively in marketing program

Loyal care - Save points

Save points on loyalty app is more convenient comparing to old style points saving on phone number and cards. For users, they can track and use their points right on the mobile app

For business, they can remind customer more often and call them back whenever they launch a new event or new product by push notification

Membership program

Efficient Online selling channel

Customer can order, apply promotion code and pay to store with safety and secured online payment .
The rest is business part to take care of the orders

For customers, they can totally control their order status, get assistant with chatbox or hotline

For business to build call center system to manage and coordinate order to shops/stores.
With intergration of delivery units, the order will be sent automatically


Booking - Table, Room and Calendar booking

Customers can book in advance table, hotel room, ... This feature helps them save time and be able to manage their booking history

Customers receive a booking code and show this code to the staff to check in

You can know whose table, room and calendar availability. In case your customer book by a third party, you still can see and control the sales and commission for your partner
