KPOS-350 Barcode label printer


KPOS-350 is 2 in 1 barcode label printer for bubble tea shop . It is the ideal product to print receipts, coupons, kitchen orders, invoices and tickets. KPOS350 is an elegant and highly performing product, designed and conceived to also suit any and all environments: be it a luxury fashion shop, a fine restaurant or a large warehouse. It is equipped with an avant-garde technological package, USB module, application notes for Windows.
