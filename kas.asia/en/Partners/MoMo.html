KAS x MoMo

KAS has successfully connected with MoMo e-wallet, customers may choose MoMo as payment method when Merchants use POS, O2O Web App... of KAS

MoMo is an e-wallet application on smartphones available on iOS and Android operating systems with more than 23 million users.

As a mobile payment platform, MoMo Wallet promotes a cashless economy and provides customers with a One Touch Payment experience with more than hundreds of service utilities, including money transfer, bills payment, airline tickets purchase, train tickets purchase, movie tickets purchase, on-behalf payments, and mobile commerce

In particular, MoMo possesses a network of financial transaction points in provinces and cities across the country, it helps millions of customers in remote areas where banking services and smartphones are not yet popular…to access financial services.
