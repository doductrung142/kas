Giao hang nhanh

Giao hang nhanh is a delivery company with extremely large scale and top delivery capacity in Vietnam. Prestigious delivery service with full of clear policies and benefits so that users may rest assured for the service.

Express delivery or express courier is a service of receiving and delivering parcels according to the time as announced in advance and calculated by hours. With express delivery service, the delivery time will be ensured but the service cost will usually be higher than other delivery service packages.

With a network covering 63 provinces, cities and more than 7000 employees, the order operation is ensured to be run smoothly, in addition, the website system and customer service also bring satisfaction. And especially, the service quality of Giao hang nhanh is very good also.
