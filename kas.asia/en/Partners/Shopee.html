Shopee Vietnam 

Shopee app is the first C2C (customer-to-customer) mobile shopping application where users can surf, shop and sell.

Products on Shopee are directly from famous, genuine brands (in Shopee Mall) or from seller channels, products have been registered with clear origin by suppliers and censored by Shopee staffs


Effective connection between customers and businesses

Connect customers and businesses closer together, easy to buy and easy to sell, possible connection with a specific user to evaluate services and refer to all available products

Fast shipping and delivery


Shopee shipping and delivery service is easy and hassle-free, Shopee protects users by providing receipts for purchase confirmation

Option for Payment method


It is flexible to choose the suitable payment method for you. Shopee ensures absolute security upon your payment by credit card, VISA or MasterCard
