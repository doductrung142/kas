Functions of fanpage
Fanpage is a page created from a personal or business Facebook account, to gather a community group with a specific interest!
The fanpage creation purpose is to do business, entertainment, branding... to connect with a community sharing a common interest.

Most of the information (images, articles, videos, tips...) you write on the fanpage will be seen by everyone in the fanpage due to the random content distribution by the Facebook system. Therefore, upon a fanpage creation, you will reach a number of people who are interested in the topic (purpose) for which you created the fanpage.

In addition, the tools and features included in the fanpage will give better support in selling, for example. In other words, fanpage helps you to measure - analyze the audience interested in your topic (age, gender, geographic reach, topics of interest ...)
