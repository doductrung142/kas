KAS x Omicall

Omicall allows users to listen - call customers, other employees with a single phone number. Businesses are required to to equip IP phones or analog phones available through Getaway, calling software (Softphone) then a professional call center with full modern features is available and ready for use.

All operations of the virtual call center are completely on the Internet, no need to equip any other system without affection to the call quality. Businesses using this service can save more than 50% of call cost compared to previously spent cost per month while using a telecommunications call center
