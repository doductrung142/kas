KAS x Stringee

Stringee is a platform (SDK/API) providing Listen - Call - Chat - SMS features, integrated into business management solution of KAS

Now, businesses may use Stringee right on Smac of KAS with following outstanding features:
- Call from App to App: Internet connection
- Call from App to Mobile Number: internet connection and GSM signal
- Call from Mobile Number to App: GSM and internet connection
- Call from the App to the call center
- Call from Mobile Number to Mobile Number through 01 virtual number to hide the caller number
- Group call
