KAS x Zalo OA

Zalo OA (Zalo Official Account) is the official information page of businesses, brands, organizations and communities on Zalo.

You can create Marketing Campaign on Zalo OA to attract customers to come to the store as quickly as possible.

KAS successfully linked to Zalo OA, we are made to be convenient and offer amazing features to businesses, such as:
1. Connect
- Smac allows you to connect to multiple Zalo OA accounts
- Manage all customer interactions through Zalo from Smac
2. Measure
- How many customers from Zalo OA
- The income from Zalo OA channel
- The sales volume from Zalo customers
3. Zalo OA Bot
- Register for new Zalo OA account
- Auto-release vouchers
- Auto-purchase, reserve & assess from customers
- Auto- care customer services on posts and messages
