Grab Food

Home/Partners/Grab Food

Your favourite food delivered hot and fresh

KAS x Grabfood: All orders from GrabFood will be synchronized automatically to your POS (merchant interface). Very time-saving and efficient for your business!

Satisfy your cravings by getting the food you love from your favourite restaurants delivered to you fast. GrabFood is now available in selected areas of Ho Chi Minh City – District 1, District 3, District 4, District 5, District 7, District 10, Phu Nhuan and Go Vap, Binh Thanh, Tan Binh. We are expanding quickly, so stay tuned for more areas soon!

Just a tap away

Feed your cravings with your favourite foods delivered to you, wherever you are

Foods for all cravings

From local fare to restaurant favourites, our wide selection of food will definitely satisfy all your cravings

Make an order
1. Launch the Grab app and select ‘Food’ from the accordion menu.
2. Fill your delivery address.
3. Select your favourite restaurant.
4. Select meal from restaurant.
